SIZE GUIDE

Denim Sizing

Our jeans are sized by waist measurement in inches. For raw/unsanforized denim:

  • Order your true waist size (jeans will be snug initially)
  • Expect 0.5-1" stretch in waist with wear
  • Unsanforized: Expect 1-2" shrinkage in waist, 2-3" in inseam after first soak
  • When between sizes, size up for comfort
