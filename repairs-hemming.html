Free Chain-Stitch Hemming

Every pair of jeans purchased from us includes free chain-stitch hemming. Just note your preferred inseam in the order comments, and we'll hem them before shipping.

Chain-stitching creates authentic vintage roping when the hem is washed—a hallmark of quality denim.
