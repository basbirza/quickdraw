OUR STORY

EST. 2026

Quickdraw Pressing Co. was born from a passion for authentic craftsmanship and a deep appreciation for the golden age of American workwear. We believe that clothing should tell a story—your story—developing character and patina with every wear.

Heritage Meets Quality

We source the finest selvedge denim from legendary Japanese mills like Kaihara, known for weaving on vintage shuttle looms that create that distinctive self-finished edge. Each jean is constructed with the same attention to detail that defined American workwear in its heyday—from copper rivets to chain-stitched hems.

Built to Last

In a world of fast fashion and planned obsolescence, we're committed to creating pieces that improve with age. Our raw denim fades uniquely to your lifestyle, our jackets develop rich patinas, and every piece is designed to be repaired, not replaced.

Our Promise

Every product we sell comes with our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer service. We stand behind our gear because we wear it ourselves. When you buy from Quickdraw, you're not just getting premium denim—you're investing in pieces that will become part of your story.
